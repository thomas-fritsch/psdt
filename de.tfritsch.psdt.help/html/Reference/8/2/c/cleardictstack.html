cleardictstack 
- cleardictstack -

pops all dictionaries off the dictionary stack except for the permanent entries. In Level 1 implementations the permanent entries are systemdict and userdict; in Level 2 they are systemdict, globaldict, and userdict. (In Level 1 implementations, cleardictstack is a procedure defined in userdict instead of an operator defined in systemdict.)

Errors: (none)
