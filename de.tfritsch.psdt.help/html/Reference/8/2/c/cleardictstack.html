cleardictstack

- cleardictstack -

pops all dictionaries off the dictionary stack except for the permanent entries. In LanguageLevel 1, the permanent entries are systemdict and userdict; in LanguageLevel 2 and 3, they are systemdict, globaldict, and userdict. (In Level 1 implementations, cleardictstack is a procedure defined in userdict instead of an operator defined in systemdict.)

Errors: none
