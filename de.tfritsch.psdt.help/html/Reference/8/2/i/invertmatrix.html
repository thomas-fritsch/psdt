invertmatrix

matrix1 matrix2 invertmatrix matrix2

replaces the value of matrix2 with the result of inverting matrix1 and pushes the result back on the operand stack. If matrix1 transforms coordinates (x, y) to (x', y') then its inverse transforms (x', y') to (x, y) (see Section 4.3.3, "Matrix Representation and Manipulation").

Errors: rangecheck, stackunderflow, typecheck, undefinedresult

See Also: itransform, idtransform
