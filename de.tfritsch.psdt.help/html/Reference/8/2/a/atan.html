atan

num den atan angle

returns the angle (in degrees between 0 and 360) whose tangent is num divided by den. Either num or den may be 0, but not both. The signs of num and den determine the quadrant in which the result will lie: a positive num yields a result in the positive y plane, a positive den yields a result in the positive x plane. The result is a real number.

Examples 

0 1 atan     ⇒  0.0
1 0 atan     ⇒  90.0
-100 0 atan  ⇒  270.0
4 4 atan     ⇒  45.0

Errors: stackunderflow, typecheck, undefinedresult

See Also: cos, sin
