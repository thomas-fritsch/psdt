deviceinfo 

- deviceinfo dict

returns a read-only dictionary containing static information about the current device. The composition of this dictionary varies according to the properties of the device. Typical entries are given in the table in section 7.3, "Graphics and Window Systems." The information in the dictionary may not be meaningful for a page-oriented or other non-display device.

The use of deviceinfo after a setcachedevice operation within the scope of a BuildChar procedure is not permitted. An undefined error results.

Errors: stackoverflow, undefined
